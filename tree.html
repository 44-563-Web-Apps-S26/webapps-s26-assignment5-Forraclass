Hog Plum

The hog plum tree (Spondias mombin or Spondias purpurea) is a fast-growing, deciduous tropical tree known for producing tangy, yellow-to-red plum-like fruits and edible young leaves. Highly productive, it thrives in full sun and warm temperatures, often used for living fences and edible landscaping. The fruits are eaten fresh, in jams, or pickled.

Pagoda Dogwood

Pagoda dogwood is an excellent native plant for the four-season garden. The unique horizontal branching pattern has a distinct tiered habit, often catching snow in the winter. Clusters of white flowers show up in spring, dark green foliage turns a beautiful burgundy-red in fall, and blue-black berries attract many birds.
